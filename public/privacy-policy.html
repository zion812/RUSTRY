RUSTRY Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

RUSTRY collects the following information to provide our poultry marketplace services:

  • Phone Number: For account creation and verification via OTP
  • Profile Information: Name, location, and farm details
  • Photos: Images of poultry, vaccination certificates, and farm documentation
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history, payment information (processed securely)
  • Device Information: For app functionality and crash reporting

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Facilitate buying and selling of poultry
  • Verify user identity and prevent fraud
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Send important notifications about your transactions

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your public profile and listings are visible to other users
  • Service Providers: Firebase, payment processors, and analytics services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure Firebase authentication and storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and data minimization

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Transaction records may be kept longer for legal and accounting purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

RUSTRY is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

Email: privacy@rustry.app

Address: RUSTRY Team, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
